Pre-Meeting Brief · Confidential

Bodic × A8N Intelligence

Bodic controls the fund's internal system of record; A8N controls the external structural intelligence that explains where conviction should come from. The question is not whether the datasets are complementary — they are — but how tightly to wire them and how fast to sell the combined stack into a market both sides already reach through Armen and Jolt.

Axis 1 — Technical Integration

Golden Source enrichment via API A8N writes company, executive, patent, and ecosystem-positioning records into Bodic's master data layer, so every module inherits external data without a separate integration.
Signals in the BI layer A8N pushes complexity-derived signals — relatedness, ecosystem momentum, competitive density — as native dashboard fields, so a partner sees external conviction drivers next to internal portfolio metrics.
A8N as an MCP-queryable source Bodic's AI agents query A8N in natural language at runtime, so an agent answering "which targets sit in an accelerating ecosystem" pulls live intelligence instead of stale extracts.
GEO Profiles × A8N Each fund's GEO Profile binds to its A8N ecosystem footprint, making the fund's AI-facing identity a verifiable market claim rather than a self-asserted description.

Axis 2 — Commercial Development

Armen portfolio as first market Run the joint product into Jolt, RGreen, Chorus, Signal, and Vendis first, proving the partnership on five reference accounts both sides already have standing to approach.
Bundled Digital + Intelligence Audit Package Bodic's infrastructure audit with A8N's positioning audit as one onboarding engagement, so a new fund buys a single diagnostic of both its data readiness and its sourcing edge.
Fund II fundraising narrative Equip GPs to tell LPs that conviction is sourced from external economic structure and executed on instrumented infrastructure, making the stack a differentiated pitch line, not a back-office cost.
Bodic Connect + SIAI 2026 List A8N in Bodic Connect and stage the joint announcement at SIAI 2026, giving the integration a dated public launch and a channel into every fund already evaluating Bodic.

Decision for the room

Deep native integration with a shared roadmap and joint go-to-market — or a listed Connect partner with a defined API contract and independent sales? And which does the Fund II timeline actually require?
