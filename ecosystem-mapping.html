How the map is built — connecting the structure of the economy

We don't rely on opinion or pitch decks. We index the largest research, IP, capital and talent datasets in the world and connect them into a single network. The signal comes from economic and technological structure: which capabilities sit next to which, who has the know-how, where knowledge is becoming dense — and therefore where a fund with your specific portfolio can realistically win. This is a prediction game on the network, not a ranking of buzzwords.
